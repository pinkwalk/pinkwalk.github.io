As Cotiviti Care strive to raise awareness about cancer, we have initiated preparations for an event named the "PinkWalk." This event is being organized in partnership with Cancer Care Nepal, a non-profit, non-governmental organization established in 2065. The primary focus of this Walkathon, particularly about breast cancer awareness, is to elevate understanding of the disease, promote early detection, foster a sense of community among those affected, and generate funding for research, treatment, and support.

Objective

The objective of a Walkathon for breast cancer awareness is to raise awareness about breast cancer, promote early detection, support individuals affected by the disease, and raise funds for research, treatment, and support services. Specifically, the objectives of a Walkathon for breast cancer awareness include:

  1. Raise Awareness: The Walkathon aims to educate the community about breast cancer, its signs and symptoms, risk factors, and the importance of early detection through regular screenings and self-examinations. By increasing awareness, the event seeks to empower individuals to take proactive measures for their breast health.
  2. Promote Early Detection and Screening: The Walkathon emphasizes the importance of early detection and regular breast cancer screenings, such as mammograms. It aims to motivate individuals to schedule screenings, educate them about available resources and support services, and encourage proactive healthcare practices for breast cancer prevention and detection.
  3. Community Engagement and Support: The Walkathon encourages community participation, fostering a sense of unity and solidarity among participants. It provides an opportunity for individuals, families, friends, and local organizations to come together, show support, and actively contribute to the cause. The event promotes community engagement and collaboration to combat breast cancer collectively.
  4. Support life after cancer: By organizing a Walkathon for breast cancer awareness, the event seeks to make a significant impact in raising awareness, promoting early detection, supporting those affected by breast cancer, and generating funds for vital research and services. The ultimate objective is to reduce the burden of breast cancer, improve outcomes, and work towards a future free from the disease.
